Crazy River Game

Crazy River

Instructions

Navigate along a randomly generated river without crashing on the shore.

The further distance that you travel along the river, the higher your score.

At set distances, the game will speed up, increasing difficulty.

Controls

Use the Left/Right keys, A/D keys, or controller buttons below the screen to accelerate you character from side-to-side.

Pro Tip: Tapping the keys instead of holding them can give you a bit more control.
